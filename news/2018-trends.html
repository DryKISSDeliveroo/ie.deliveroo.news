DELIVEROO REVEALS THE TOP 100 DISHES ORDERED IN 2018 AROUND THE WORLD

  • 12 December 2018
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 22 min
dublin deliveroo trends 2018
Share this article
  • Irish restaurants shine bright in Top 100

As the year comes to a close, food delivery service Deliveroo takes a moment to reflect on the food highlights of 2018 and reveal the most popular dishes around the world, as chosen by its customers.  From bubble tea to burritos - this year's eclectic mix of take-away favourites certainly makes for an interesting read. Better still, eight Irish restaurants make the global 100.

Ireland's representation on the list shows our love of fusion food and fresh ingredients.

Ireland's showing on the global list includes:

4) Burrito from Boojum, Dublin

25) Chicken Tenders from Camden Rotisserie, Dublin

35) Satay (Wok Dish) from Xian Street Food, Galway

43) The Palestinian Falafel (V) from Umi Falafel, Dublin

70) Chicken Massaman Curry from Saba, Dublin

81) Ebi Gyoza from Musashi, Dublin

83) Scoop Gelato Box from Scoop, Dublin

88) Chicken Tenders from Rocket's, Dublin

Here's a taster of this year's overall top ten dishes on Deliveroo around the world:

1) Pad Thaï from Thaï at Home, Paris

2) Cheeseburger from Five Guys, London

3) Sushi Lovers Poke Bowl from CALI-POKE, Dubai

4) Burrito from Boojum, Dublin

5) Bubble Tea from Tenren's Tea, Hong Kong

6) Grilled Chicken Burrito from Guzman Y Gomez, Sydney

7) Burrito from Gonzalez & Co, Barcelona

8) Beef Burger from Tommi's Burger Joint, Berlin

9) Poke Bowl from Temakery, Amsterdam

10) 2 Hot Wings® from KFC, Brighton

While noodles and Hawaiian poke bowls were trending this year, 2018 also saw healthy plant-based dishes grow in popularity.  A firm favourite in last year, pizza remained a top choice for food delivery around the world with five appearing in the top 100.

Deliveroo also reports an increase in orders of more traditional takeaway cuisines, with favourites including a Doner Kebab from German Doner Kebab in Birmingham and Satay from Xian Street Food in Galway.

A closer look at Deliveroo's top 100 reveals some fun foodie ordering trends over the course of 2018.

Health Kick

With orders of healthy options almost tripling this year, it's no surprise dishes like The Original vegan Açaí Bowl from the UAE's The Acai Spot or Singapore's Harvest Protein & Grain Bowl from Harvest have made it into the top 100. The most frequently occuring dish in the list, featuring twelve times, from eight different countries is the incredibly instagrammable and equally nutritious Poke Bowl.

Comfort Foods

But it's not all raw fish and salads. Our classic comfort foods - burgers and burritos - fill almost a fifth of the spots on this year's Deliveroo 100, with favourites including Dublin's Boojum Burrito and Germany's Classic Burger from Tommi's Burger Joint.

Sweet Treats

It seems people have also developed a sweeter tooth this year, with five times more desserts appearing in this years top 100 compared to last, including the Banana Pudding from Dubai-based Magnolia Bakery and Berlin's Happy Waffle from Waffle Brothers.

Local Favourites

This year's top 100 also showed that people are celebrating their local favourites, in addition to the popular high street chains. Favourite national dishes include Tortilla Española from Madrid-based José Luis, Shrimp Dumplings from Dim Dim Sum in Hong Kong and Boulette Sauce Brusseleir from Ballekes in Brussels.
