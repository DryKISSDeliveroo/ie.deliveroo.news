Ordering Deliveroo this week could earn you €1,000 with €5K in Five Days Giveaway

  • 4 November 2018
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 2 min
dublin deliveroo
Share this article
  • Customers who order this week could win themselves €1,000 cash
  • One winner will be chosen at random each day from Monday to Friday

Couch to 5K? More like €5K in 5 days. This week, Deliveroo is giving away a €1,000 Deliveroo Gold Card to one lucky customer each day from Monday to Friday to celebrate the delivery platform's 1,000th restaurant partnership in Ireland.

That's right - ordering food can reward you with even more food. With Winter already on our doorsteps, it could be made a whole lot better with €1,000 cash.

The food delivery platform's partnership with Ramen Asian Street Food, Ennis Road, Limerick has marked the 1000th restaurant partner sign up for Deliveroo in Ireland.

One Deliveroo order per day will contain a special Deliveroo Gold Card, which the customer will be able to exchange for €1,000. 

Deliveroo launched in Ireland in 2015, and in that time has partnered with many of Ireland's favourite restaurants across Dublin, Cork, Galway and Limerick and Belfast. The platform is known for its innovative technology, allowing customers to track their orders from restaurant to front door.

Liam Cox, Regional Manager with Deliveroo Ireland said: "Deliveroo has received a very warm welcome in Ireland for its delicious food offering and excellent delivery service, so we want to celebrate by giving back to our customers with 5K in 5 days.

"One lucky customer each day will find a Deliveroo Gold Card placed inside their Deliveroo order at random", he added.

To have a chance of winning, customers just need to order on Deliveroo at any time between Monday 5th November and Friday the 10th of November. Customers who find a gold Deliveroo card included in their order will just need to email goldcard@deliveroo.ie to claim their prize.
